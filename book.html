Workshops

Daily workshops are presented by a number of specialists in the fields of art, reading, writing, publishing, bookmaking, and translation, which aim to hone skills and spread knowledge awareness to visitors of the Riyadh International Book Fair.

he riyadh international book fair is accompanied by a number of qualitative cultural events and programs, targeting all segments of society and forming a cultural window through which the world learns about saudi culture.

Various cultural and literary seminars, as well as poetry evenings featuring elite Nabati and Al Fasih poets, which will be held on the main stage and the talk platform of the Riyadh International Book Fair. (Program schedule)
